Welcome to the ChatGPT Playlist Generator


How To Use:

Enter your request to ChatGPT below in the "playlist request" field. You can ask ChatGPT to make you any kind of playlist such as: "I want you to create a playlist of music similar to the Deftones song Cherry Waves."

Treat this request as you would normally using ChatGPT in your browser. Then add your playlist name in its field and then hit the generate button.
