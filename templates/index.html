How to Use

How to Use

Enter your request to ChatGPT above in the "playlist request" field. You can ask ChatGPT to make you any kind of playlist such as: "I want you to create a playlist of music similar to the Deftones song Cherry Waves." By default, around 10 songs will be chosen but the user can specify the length of playlist in minutes/amount of songs as part of thier prompt.

Then add your playlist name in its field and then hit the generate button.

You will then be directed to a page with your Spotify playlist. You can click on the playlist here and be redirected to its Spotify page. All playlists are created on my account and can be saved/duplicated by the user to the user's personal account. Unfortunatley due the nature of Spotify's API, I cannot have the playlist created on the users account as it requires an API account and its respective keys to do so.

For those who use Apple Music, you can download the free app SongShift on your phone and convert these playlists through the app.
